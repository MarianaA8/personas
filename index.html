The People Who Inspire Me

Keyle Jenner

Kylie Kristen Jenner is an American businesswoman, socialite, designer and model. She achieved some notoriety in the pink press and on television shows as a result of her appearance on the Kardashian family's self-promotional reality show, Keeping Up with the Kardashian.

Post Malone

Austin Richard Post, better known by his stage name Post Malone, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter and producer. He gained widespread recognition in August 2015.

Jennifer Lynn Lopez Rodríguez

Jennifer Lynn Lopez Rodríguez, better known as Jennifer Lopez or J.Lo, is an American actress, singer, dancer, songwriter, record producer, fashion designer, businesswoman, television producer, choreographer and perfumer.
